Waffles at Home

Craving your favourite foods but unwilling to leave the house?

You've come to the right place! Below you'll find the perfect recipe for the fluffiest waffles imaginable that will make you wish you never left the house in the first place!

Overview of Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 3 tbsps of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Put on your favourite tunes: I'm currently listening to Spotify's Nightcap.
  2. Preheat your waffle iron, spray with non stick cooking spray and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. In a medium bowl, beat the egg whites with a hand mixer (or a strong arm) until stiff peaks form. Set aside.
  5. In a separate medium bowl, mix the egg yolks, vegetable oil, milk, and vanilla extract.
  6. Add the egg yolk mixture to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  7. Fold in the egg whites.
  8. Pour the batter onto your hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  9. Serve with butter, syrup, fresh berries or any of your favourite toppings.
