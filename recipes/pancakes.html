Pancakes

Need a reason to wake up in the morning?

Pancakes are surely the answer and with this recipe in your arsenal, you'll never want to sleep in again!

Overview of Ingredients

  • 100g of plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300 ml of semi-skimmed milk
  • 1 tbsp of vegetable or sunflower oil, with extra for frying
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Add 100g of plain flour and 1 pinch of salt into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Make a well in the centre and crack 2 eggs into the middle.
  3. Pour in 50ml of semi-skimmed milk and 1 tbsp oil.
  4. Whisk from the centre, gradually drawing the flour into the eggs, milk, and oil. Once all the flour is incorporated, beat until you have a smooth, thick paste. Add a little more milk if it is too stiff to beat.
  5. Add a generous splash of milk and whisk to loosen the thick batter. While whisking, pour in a steady stream of the remaining milk. Continue pouring and whisking until you have a batter with a consistency of slightly thick single cream.
  6. Heat your pan, then wipe it with oiled kitchen paper.
  7. Ladle some batter into the pan, tilting the pan to move the mixture for a thin and even layer.
  8. Leave to cook, undisturbed, for approx. 30 seconds. If the pan is the right temperature, the pancake should turn golden underneath after 30 seconds and will be ready to turn.
  9. Ease a palette knife under the pancake and flip it over. Cook for another 30 seconds.
  10. Continue with the rest of the better, serving them as you cook.
  11. Add your favourite toppings, sweet or savoury, for the ultimate pancake experience.
